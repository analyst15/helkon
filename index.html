Who We Are

Helkon (K) Ltd has been operational in Kenya since 1998. It is situated at Ramco Court, Bellevue, Mombasa Road, Nairobi with branches in Mombasa at Social Security House 11th Floor House, Nkurumah Road and Kisumu at KCB Building.
Our business concept is to provide prompt & quality services. We are environmentally friendly, legally compliant and socially responsible corporate company.

  • Vision

    To be the leading service provider within East Africa, providing world class cleaning, waste management, landscaping and hospitality services.

  • Mission

    Provision of affordable and quality services to our clients through continuous monitoring, identifying their needs and engaging them to promptly deliver, meet and exceed their expectations, thereby adding value.

  • Core Values

  • Care - We take care of our staff, clients, stakeholders and the environment.
  • Credibility - We keep our promise.
  • Reliability - We respond to our client needs positively and promptly.
  • Integrity - We adhere to the standards embodied by our code of ethics.
  • Loyalty - We give 100% to the attainment of client satisfaction in line with our company objectives.
