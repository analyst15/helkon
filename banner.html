Who We Are

Helkon (K) Ltd has been operational in Kenya since 1998. It is situated at Barclay House, Mai Mahiu Rd, off Langata Road with branches in Mombasa at Karimi House, Moi Avenue and Kisumu at KCB Building Our business concept is to provide prompt & quality services with professionalism that is environmentally- friendly and efficient.
