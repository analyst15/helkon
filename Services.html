House Keeping and Kitchen Cleaning Services

  1. Facility Cleaning

We provide a healthy environment for your patients and staff. Most importantly, we ensure a sterile area safe for everyone. With loads of experience in the field, we are capable of handling your specialized needs. We believe that it is imperative to use only the services of a professional Hospital Cleaning company in order to avoid contamination. We have a well-trained team of professionals with knowledge and understanding of:-

  1. Hospital grade disinfectants
  2. Dilution rates
  3. Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS)
  4. Colour Codes
  5. Infection control & Use of proper personal preventive equipment
  6. Correct segregation and disposal of wastes

    1. Commercial Kitchen Cleaning Services

    We provide reliable and experienced kitchen cleaning staff. Our dedication towards this responsibility is to provide utmost customer satisfaction. Our cleaning team will ensure ingrained dirt and grease is cleaned from commercial kitchen floors. Cooker hoods, fixtures and fittings, walls, cooking equipment and cleaning equipment are all cleaned, managed and maintained well. We only use carefully selected products to ensure grease and dirt is removed effectively, whilst paying close attention to company policies and legislation regarding environmental impact

    Waste Management and Disposal Services

    We endeavor to ensure that our environment is clean and with consistent proper waste management practices there is an opportunity for our clients and the society at large to reap a range of benefits from a cleaner /greener environment. We are licensed to offer garbage collection & disposal services and we are also NEMA compliant. We also offer solution-based waste collection & disposal services, this ranges from disposal of all kinds of plastics, papers etc. We use the following disposal methods:

    1. Incineration.
    2. Recycling.
    3. Biological
    4. Shredding

    Patients Attendants & Pottering Services

    We provide these essential frontline services within hospitals. We offer a wide range of activities across the hospital premises. Essential services provided by the Portering team include:

    1. Transfer of patients between departments within the hospital
    2. Transfer of patient specimens to the laboratory
    3. Assisting nursing staff in patient lifting and positioning etc
    4. Front desk services

    Gardeening & Grounds Maintenance Services

    3. Hard & Soft Landscaping
